# PYEXPO2K26

**PYEXPO2K26** is a collaborative innovation-focused event powered by the **IPS Community**, designed to empower students to transform their ideas into real, impactful projects.

This expo serves as a platform where creativity meets execution, encouraging students to ideate, build, collaborate, and showcase innovative solutions using modern technologies.

---

## About the Event

PYEXPO2K26 brings together passionate students, mentors, and the IPS community to foster a culture of innovation, problem-solving, and hands-on learning.

Participants work collaboratively to:
- Explore real-world problems  
- Convert ideas into working prototypes  
- Learn through peer collaboration and mentorship  
- Present their projects to a wider audience  

---

## Objectives

- Encourage student-driven innovation  
- Promote collaborative project development  
- Provide exposure to practical and real-world problem solving  
- Build confidence in idea pitching and technical execution  
- Strengthen the student tech community  

---

## What Students Will Do

- Brainstorm and propose innovative ideas  
- Form teams and collaborate effectively  
- Develop projects using suitable technologies  
- Document and present their work  
- Receive feedback and recognition  

---

## Powered By

**IPS Community**  
A student-centric community focused on learning, collaboration, and technical growth.
